Attribute Setup

Stat or state of a character or item. For example, it could be something like these:

  • Strength — character trait used to determine outcomes
  • Damage Bonus — multiplier for an item
  • Current HP — state
  • Max HP — character statistic, or item stat that affects the character stat
  • Poisoned — temporary status condition, a number that goes to 0 after a few turns
  • Satiation — status such as Very Full (95) that slowly decreases over time
  • Posture — state such as standing or crawling

    Alternatively, it may be easier to set up an item to represent a status that needs to be incremented, such as the remaining HP of a tree to be felled.
